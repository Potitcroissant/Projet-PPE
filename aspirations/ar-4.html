Arabic

[edit]

Noun

[edit]

نَظْرَة • (naẓra) f (dual نَظْرَتَان (naẓratān), plural نَظَرَات (naẓarāt))

  1. instance noun of نَظَرَ (naẓara): a look
    أَلْقَى نَظْرَةً ― ʔalqā naẓratan ― he had a look
  2. perspective
