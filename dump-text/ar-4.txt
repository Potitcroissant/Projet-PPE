Arabic

   [edit]

Noun

   [edit]

   نَظْرَة • (naẓra) f (dual نَظْرَتَان (naẓratān), plural نَظَرَات
   (naẓarāt))
    1. instance noun of نَظَرَ (naẓara): a look

                أَلْقَى نَظْرَةً ― ʔalqā naẓratan ― he had a look

    2. perspective

Etymology

   [edit]

   Learned borrowing from Arabic نَظْرَة (naẓra).

Pronunciation

   [edit]
     * IPA^(key): /nazˤ.ra/, /naðˤ.ra/

Noun

   [edit]

   نظرة • (naẓra) f (plural نَظْرات (naẓrāt) or نَظَرات (naẓarāt))
    1. a look

                Synonym: شوفة (šōfa)
